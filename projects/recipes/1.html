Bird Nest Cookies

    Ingredients

  • 1, 12oz pkg butterscotch chips
  • 1 Tablespoon Crisco shortening
  • 5 cups chow mien noodles
  • 1, 12oz pkg M&Ms

Procedure

  1. Melt shortening and butterscotch chips until creamy
  2. Stir in chow mien noodles
  3. Spoon mixture onto wax paper in desired cookie size
  4. Add M&Ms to the center of cookie before cookies solidify
