Bird Nest Cookies

This is a family recipe from my grandmother. Fun fact, my father made this recipe and won a gift card to McDonalds and his picture in a newspaper. Winning tastes good -my father who won 2nd
This is a no bake recipe, making it very easy to do

Ingredients Directions
1, 12 oz pkg butterscotch chips Melt shortening and butterscotch chips until creamy
1 Tablespoon Crisco shortening Stir in chow mien noodles
5 cups chow mien noodles Spoon mixture onto wax paper in desired cookie size
1, 12 oz pkg M&Ms Add M&Ms to the center of cookie before cookies solidify

Tips and Tricks

  • When shaping the "nests" clump them together and make a divot for the M&Ms
  • Can replace M&Ms with Milkduds or other round candy
  • Be careful of the butterscotch! It makes the chow mien hot and sticky
  • Cookies solidify fast, so add M&Ms quickly
