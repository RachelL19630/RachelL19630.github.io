Peanut Butter Blossoms

This recipe is also a family recipe. Someone (I believe either my mother or grandmother found this recipe in the magazine.) My family would make this every christmas to leave out for Santa as well as enjoy ourselves. I liked them a lot, but I do not like chocolate, so I would eat them without the chocolate kiss -Kaitlyn

Ingredients Directions
1/2 Cup Crisco® Butter Flavor All-Vegetable Shortening HEAT oven to 375°F
1/2 cup Jif® Creamy Peanut Butter Cream together shortening, peanut butter, brown sugar and 1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup firmly packed brown sugar Add egg, milk and vanilla
1/2 cup granulated sugar Beat well
1 large egg STIR together flour, baking soda and salt
2 tablespoons milk Add to creamed mixture
1 teaspoon vanilla extract Beat on low speed until stiff dough forms
1 3/4 cups Pillsbury BEST® All Purpose flour Shape into 1-inch balls
1 teaspoon baking soda Roll in sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt Place 2 inches apart on ungreased cookie sheet
Sugar BAKE for 10 to 12 minutes or until golden brown
48 foil-wrapped milk chocolate pieces unwrapped Top each cookie immediately with an unwrapped chocolate piece, pressing down firmly so tht the cookie cracks around the edge
Remove cookie sheet to cool

YIELD: 4 dozen

Tips and Tricks

  • Make one inch ball and measure it, then use that ball as an example for all the others
  • It is best to use the original chocolate pieces, fancy flavors usually cause problems
  • Place the chocolate pieces right after the cookies come out of the oven so that the chocolate pieces will melt
